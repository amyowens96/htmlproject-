Frequently Asked Questions





Workvivo is an internal communication's platform for medium to large organisations that has a strong focus on improving employee engagement and bringing the organisations goals to life. Many companies are using Workvivo to communicate updates across their business, enabling their employees to recognise their peers and highlight how the work people do every day has an impact on the goals and objectives of the business.
How you login to Workvivo depends on whether your workplace is using Single Sign On (SSO) for login. If they are, you simply go to Workvivo and you will be automatically logged in ‐ your account is tied to the same account you use to login to your computer, access email and other services. If they are not using SSO, you simply navigate to the Workvivo address for your employer and enter your work email address and Workvivo password to login.

You can also login to Workvivo on an iOS or Android mobile device. Go to the App Store or Google Play Store and search for ″Workvivo″. Once you have downloaded the app, enter your work email address and you'll be guided through logging in for the first time.
If your workplace is using Single Sign On, you don't have a Workvivo password ‐ your account is tied to the same account you use for logging into your computer and accessing other services. If you're having trouble accessing Workvivo and your company uses SSO you should contact your internal IT support team for assistance.

If your workplace is not using SSO, you can set up a new password by clicking on the ″Forgot Your Password?″ link on the Workvivo login screen and entering your work email address. We will immediately send you an email with a special link you can use to change your password.
When an organisation starts using Workvivo, we send out an email to every employee set up on the system with instructions on accessing Workvivo. If you did not receive an email and you think you should have, reach out to our support team at support@workvivo.com and we'll check for you.

The activity feed is the central hub of activity in the Workvivo platform. When any content is posted or anything happens in Workvivo, it will appear on the activity feed. This includes everything from posts and shout-outs, to work anniversary and new hire welcome messages, to newly published news articles, events and much more. For most users, the activity feed is where they spend most of their time engaging with Workvivo.
To post an update, tap or click on the ″Post an Update″ button at the top of the activity feed. This will open a screen where you can add text, images, videos, links, attachments and more. You can also select an audience for your posts, if you don't want the entire company to see it. You can link a post to a goal, or alternatively post a shout‐out.
A shout‐out is an update where an employee is thanking or recognising one of their peers. Before Workvivo, people would often give shout‐outs in person or perhaps by email ‐ which tend to get lost in time and don't encourage any interaction by other staff. In Workvivo, shout‐outs allow you to recognise colleagues in a new way that is highly visible across the business, and facilitates others to join in the celebration with likes, comments and more.
Absolutely! You can tag as many colleagues as you like on a shout‐out. When you tag a user in a shout‐out, they will get a notification that they received a shout‐out ‐ so they'll know straight away that you've recognised their efforts!
Sure ‐ you can tag individual people, teams or any combination of people and teams. This is really useful for when a project has just finished, a team has achieved something great or for any other positive message you want to share.
When posting an update, simply tap or click the ″Link this update to a goal″ option to open a window that lets you browse your company's goals. You can also link an update to a team goal ‐ this will only be visible to other member's of that team.
To search in Workvivo, simply click on the search icon in the navigation bar. Alternatively, you can hit the backslash key on your keyboard regardless of where you are in Workvivo, and the search box will appear. When you search in Workvivo, it will look for content across the entire platform ‐ updates, shout‐outs, articles, events, goals, spaces, questions, teams and even people. This makes it really easy to find what you are looking for ‐ and fast!
